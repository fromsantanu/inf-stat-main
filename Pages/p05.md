## âœ¨ **3. Spearman Rank Correlation**

âœ… **Use when:**

* Data is **ordinal** or **not normally distributed**
* **Monotonic** but not necessarily linear relationship

ðŸ”§ **Range:** -1 to +1 (similar interpretation to Pearson)

âœ”ï¸ **Example:** Relationship between math and physics ranks in class.

ðŸ”§ **Python Example:**

```python

```

âœ… **Interpretation:**

* **p-value < 0.05:** Significant monotonic relationship.
